Project Monkey is the place where idea geniuses and budding developers come together to form solid mobile development teams.

Have an idea and need a team around you to help develop it? No problem.

Feel you have unexploited development skills and want to get involved in a new mobile app project?
No problem.

Project Monkey is the place to begin!

Build a solid team, by meeting developers and interacting with them to find out whether you feel you would work well together.

You market your project idea to help attract other developers, stating as much (or as little) about the project as you wish!

You get to approve the developers you decide you wish to work with, and have a range of tools at your disposal to help screen through the many different developers available.

FAQ

Who is this website aimed at?
This website is aimed at both people with ideas, who wish to build a team, as well as developers who wish to be part of a team.
What kind of projects are supported?
The website is targeted towards, but not limited to mobile app projects. Have a web app idea? Great! Have a game idea? No problem! Any type of development project is supported at Project Monkey.
